About Clara

A guide for women
coming home
to themselves.

I'm Clara — somatic guide, space holder, and Projector in Human Design. I work with women who feel stuck in survival mode, burned out, and disconnected from themselves.

My work is body-based and unhurried. We move at the pace your nervous system can actually handle — because real change doesn't come from pushing harder. It comes from finally feeling safe enough to let go.

I help women rebuild safety in their bodies so they can trust themselves, reclaim their confidence, and feel like themselves again.
